About Me

Hello! I'm Anuj, an engineer with a dual passion for building scalable data pipelines and developing cutting-edge generative AI models. My journey in tech began with a fascination for how data can be structured and utilized, which naturally led me to the world of data engineering. I've designed and implemented ETL/ELT processes, built data warehouses, and ensured data quality for large-scale applications.

More recently, I've dived deep into the realm of Generative AI. From fine-tuning Large Language Models (LLMs) to building Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems and deploying AI-powered applications, I thrive on bridging the gap between raw data and intelligent, creative outputs. I believe the most powerful AI systems are built on a foundation of clean, reliable, and well-architected data.
